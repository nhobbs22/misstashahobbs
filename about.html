About Me

Miss Hobbs

My name is Natasha Hobbs. I am from the Bay Area, California! I received my Bachelors in Studio Art as well as a minor musical theater at Santa Clara University back in 2012. I also received my Masters in Teaching at Santa Clara in 2022. Go Broncos! When I'm not at school, I enjoy hiking, going to the beach, or visiting the river. I also love painting, singing karaoke, and spending time with my wonderful family and friends.

My previous professional backgrounds include: makeup artist, UX/UI designer, and graphic designer. While I learned alot from my previous careers, I am so excited for this opportunity to teach. I believe that all children are capable of greatness and I am committed to provide them with the means to reach their true potential.

My Teaching Philosophy

    Each and every one of us have our own bit of magic, and I believe that is my responsibility as a future educator to help find what makes a child sparkle and encourage that child to shine to their brightest potential. No two students work the same way and what motivates each individual is very different. I want to create a classroom experience tailored to help students’ individualities and differences shine. This can be accomplished by creating a supportive and inclusive community in my classroom where students feel safe to express their ideas.

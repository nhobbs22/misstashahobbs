Rules & Procedures

I have one big rule in my class room and it is that it is 100% TOTALLY OKAY to be wrong in my class AS LONG AS you are always TRYING! It is okay if you make mistakes. Mistakes are an important part of learning! I just want you to always try your very best. I have faith in every one of my students. Let's make magic happen!

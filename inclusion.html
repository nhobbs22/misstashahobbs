Inclusion

Considerations

English Language Learners

I will develop vocabulary for English Language Learners (ELLs) by creating pictionary games and using contextual guesswork. I will also create keywords for readings with definitions attached and introduce vocabulary daily. I will give students points for using those words in the day's activities.

Gifted Students

If a student is appearing to be understimulated, I will ensure that they are provided with rigorous instruction that amplifies our daily lesson plan. I want to ensure that all of my students have the opportunity to to learn in their zone of proximal development.

Diverse Learners

I understand that each child thinks, processes, and learns differently. Using UDL, I will ensure that I provide ample visual and auditory instruction as well as hands-on activities. I will follow guidelines for IEPs and make sure to provide the necessary accommodations that students need based on their diagnosis.

Socioeonomically Disadvantaged

If you are struggling to pay for supplies or materials, please let me know so I can ensure that your child gets the materials they need. We will have an amazon wishlist set up where parents can donate by purchasing supplies on our list. The materials your child needs will be added anonymously.

Collaborations

Resource Specialist

I will collaborate with the Resource Specialists to ensure that any student with Individualized Education Programs are getting the help they need and are being taught the skills necessary to perform at their first grade level expectations.

Principal and Administration

I will collaborate with the Principal and Administrative Staff to ensure that our classroom goals are in line with our school goals. I will make sure that our classroom is involved with the entire school community so that we all function together as a unit.

Attend District Board Meetings

I plan on attending district board meetings to ensure that I am up to date with any policy that may directly impact my classroom. I am devoted to being an advocate for my students in every way that I can.

Classroom Demographics

Our classroom this year consists of 24 wonderfully diverse students! We are so lucky to have students of various ethnic backgrounds ranging from 7 Latin American students, 8 White students, 5 African American students, and 4 Asian American students . There are 13 girls and 11 boys in our class this year. Our students speak various languages and I am excited to include all of them in our lessons. We have: English, Spanish, and Japanese.
